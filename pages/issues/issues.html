Language Support Issues

Haskell Support

We have identified an unintended oversight with Haskell support that we plan to fix as soon as we get some spare cycles this weekend. We apologize for the inconvenience. You are welcome to submit with any of the other supported languages, or simply hang tight on your solve submissions until support is restored. Thanks again for your understanding.

Language Requests

We try our best to support as many languages as we have an active community of player interest. Currently C# and then Java have been commonly requested, and we will keep you posted as soon as we can make implementations on our infrastructure to support. As always, if you would like to see a language supported that we don't offer, let an admin know. Thanks.

Password Reset

We've had a few reports of issues with the password reset feature. For some individuals this has been a mistake of trying to log in to the persistent site at https://codewarz.ninja/ instead of the live competition site at https://live.codewarz.ninja/ . Please ensure you are logging in to the right site for the account you are trying to access. They are completely separate.

During our next maintenance cycle we will take a look at the password reset feature to ensure it is functioning properly.

In the mean time, send an email to an admin or dm us on slack.
